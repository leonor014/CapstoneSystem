Top Tourist Spots in Sagada

Explore the natural and cultural wonders of Sagada

Sumaguing Cave

Known as the "Big Cave," Sumaguing Cave offers a thrilling spelunking experience with breathtaking rock formations and cool underground pools.

  • 10 minutes ride from town center
  • 40 to 45 minutes' walk from town
  • 1 to 2 hours exploration

    • GUIDE FEE: P800.00 for 5 pax
    SHUTTLE FEE: P400.00 for 10 pax

Lumiang Cave

Known for its traditional burial site, Lumiang Cave features coffins stacked at the cave’s entrance, reflecting ancient burial practices. The cave also connects to Sumaguing Cave, forming part of the famous cave connection adventure.

  • 20 to 30 minutes' walk from town
  • Enter at Lumiang Cave then exit at Sumaguing Cave (Cave Connection)
  • 5 to 10 minutes ride from town
  • 3 to 4 hours exploration

    • GUIDE FEE: P1500/ 3 PAX
    SHUTTLE FEE: 400/ 10 PAX

Balangagan Cave

A less frequented cave offering a serene spelunking experience with stunning stalactites, stalagmites.

  • 1 hour walk from town
  • 20 minutes ride from town
  • 1 to 1.5 hour of exploration

    • GUIDE FEE: P650/ 5 PAX
    SHUTTLE FEE: 650/ 10 PAX

Saint Mary the Virgin Church

Built in 1904, this historic Episcopal church features beautiful architecture and serves as a significant cultural and religious landmark in Sagada.

Sagada Cemetery

The Sagada Cemetery, especially during the Panag-apoy Festival, provides a glimpse into local traditions and the community's reverence for their ancestors.

Echo Valley and Hanging Coffins

A unique cultural landmark, the hanging coffins in Echo Valley represent an ancient burial tradition that is still practiced by some locals today.

Baw-eng Coffee Farm

A must-visit for coffee enthusiasts, this farm showcases the local coffee-growing process and offers a chance to taste the rich Arabica coffee Sagada is known for.

Latang Undergroud River

A unique subterranean river surrounded by lush vegetation, offering an adventure that combines trekking and exploring an enchanting waterway.

Bomod-ok Falls

Also known as the "Big Falls," this majestic waterfall requires a scenic trek through rice terraces and villages, offering an unforgettable adventure.

  • 30-minute ride from town to Brgy. Bangaan (drop off)
  • 45 mintes to 1 hour walk from drop off to the site

    • GUIDE FEE: P500 for 7 pax
    SHUTTLE FEE:

    • P650 Bangaan drop off and fetch
    • P900 Bangaan drop off and fetch at Barangay Pide

Bokong Falls

Known as the "Small Falls," Bokong Falls is a quaint and accessible spot ideal for a quick dip and relaxation.

  • 30 minutes’ walk from town to the falls

    • GUIDE FEE: P300 for 10 pax

Pongas Falls

A serene waterfall located off the beaten path, Pongas Falls offers a quiet retreat and picturesque scenery.

  • 30-minute ride from town to Penged, Ankileng
  • 1 hour hike to the falls

    • GUIDE FEE: P600 for 10 pax
    SHUTTLE FEE: P650 for 10 pax

Sagada Weaving

Discover the traditional art of weaving in Sagada, where you can buy authentic handwoven products directly from the makers.
*PRIVATELY OWNED*

Ganduyan Museum

This museum offers a glimpse into the history, culture, and artifacts of Sagada and the surrounding region.
*PRIVATELY OWNED*

Sagada Pottery

Learn about the art of pottery-making from skilled artisans and try your hand at creating your own ceramic masterpiece.
*PRIVATELY OWNED*

Marlboro

Famous for its rolling hills and spectacular sunrise views, Marlboro Hills is a must-visit spot for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

  • 25 to 30 minutes’ walk from the town
  • 45 minutes to 1 hour hike from drop off

    • GUIDE FEE: P800.00 for 10 pax
    SHUTTLE FEE: P650.00 for 10 pax

Marlboro to Blue Soil

  • 4 to 5 hours mountain traverse

    • GUIDE FEE: P1,600.00 for 10 pax
    SHUTTLE FEE: P1,300.00 for 10 pax

Blue Soil

Also known as Kaman-utek Hills, this site features strikingly blue-tinted soil caused by high copper sulfate content, making it a unique natural wonder.

  • 30 to 45 minutes hike from the drop off

    • GUIDE FEE: P800.00 for 10 pax
    SHUTTLE FEE: P650.00 for 10 pax

Blue Lagoon

A picturesque hidden gem with vibrant blue waters, perfect for relaxing and enjoying nature’s serenity.

Ampacao

A popular hiking destination offering panoramic views of Sagada and its surrounding mountains. It’s an ideal spot for nature lovers and sunrise or sunset chasers.

  • Sunrise/Sunset Viewing
  • 1 hour hike from Ambasing Elementary School

    • GUIDE FEE: P800.00 for 10 pax

Nabas-ang To Ampacao

  • 2 to 3 hours hike
  • Atowanan to Nabas-ang to Ampacao to Ambasing

    • GUIDE FEE: P1,200.00 for 10 pax

Pog-o Viewpoint

A scenic viewpoint providing breathtaking vistas of Sagada’s terraced rice fields, lush forests, and the mountain landscape.
*PRIVATELY OWNED*

Langsayan

A serene and lesser-known spot ideal for relaxation and quiet reflection, surrounded by Sagada’s rich greenery and peaceful ambiance.

  • 3-hours hike
  • From town to center to danum viewpoint to Palidan, Bangaan to town center

    • GUIDE FEE: P1,200.00 for 10 pax
    SHUTTLE FEE: P650.00 for 10 pax
