About Sagada

Sagada is a serene town located in the Mountain Province of the Philippines. Known for its stunning landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and adventure-filled activities, it has become a favorite destination for travelers seeking tranquility and natural beauty. From exploring the ancient hanging coffins to trekking through the lush mountains and witnessing breathtaking sunrises at Kiltepan Viewpoint, Sagada offers a unique experience that resonates with nature and history lovers alike.
